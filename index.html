About

A restless mind between Tokyo and the internet

I'm Zyal. Most days I work in healthcare marketing, translating complex ideas into stories that actually connect. But when the laptop stays open past midnight, it's because I'm chasing a thread: wiring up an AI experiment, sketching a new project, or writing something I'll probably post on Rednote before breakfast.

I'm happiest in the overlap, where marketing meets making, where a weekend prototype teaches more than any course, and where the best ideas come from following your curiosity instead of a plan.

All of these pieces are created and produced by me. They are fragments of my life, shaped by love, memories, and moments across different places and times.

Some were inspired by my experiences in Montenegro and Spain. Some capture the quiet emotions of job hunting alone in Japan, walking the same commuting routes day after day.

A few Chinese songs date back to my middle school years. The lyrics came from an essay collection, while the melodies were improvised — and once performed in front of my entire class.

These works may not be perfect, but they are honest and deeply personal. I hope you enjoy this collection of creations that carry a piece of my journey.
